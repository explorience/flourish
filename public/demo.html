The idea

One place where anyone in London can post what they need, or what they have to offer. Neighbours see it. Someone responds. Things happen.

No algorithm. No ads. No account required to browse.

Privacy

Built with care

  • No account required to browse or respond
  • No addresses ever stored or shown — only approximate area
  • Phone numbers stored privately, never displayed publicly
  • Private in-app messaging between poster and responder
  • No tracking. No ads. No algorithmic feed.
How it works

Simple by design

1
Someone posts a need or offer
Via the website, or by texting us — no account required
2
A neighbour sees it and responds
Browse the board, filter by type or category, find something you can help with
3
They connect privately
In-app messaging keeps phone numbers and emails off the public board
4
The post is marked fulfilled
The community sees what's working. Gratitude travels.
Who it's for

"Anyone who has ever needed something and didn't know who to ask — and anyone who has something to give and didn't know where to start."

The newcomer

Just moved here. Doesn't yet have a network. Needs a hand — or wants to offer skills.

The elder

Has things to give. Wants connection. Doesn't want an app — just a phone call or a text.

The neighbour

Has a bit of time. Wants to help. Didn't know anyone nearby needed anything.

Not another Facebook group

Different by choice

✕
No algorithm deciding what you see
Posts are sorted by freshness. That's it.
✕
No engagement metrics or likes
Success is a need met, not a post going viral.
✕
No corporate ownership of your relationships
The connections you make belong to you, not a platform.
✓
Community-owned, community-run
Flourish exists to serve London. That's the whole brief.
The vision

What this could become

→
Neighbourhood-level boards
Wortley Village has different needs than Hyde Park. Let communities shape their own feed.
→
Community organizations posting on behalf of members
Food banks, shelters, immigrant services — direct pipeline to neighbours who can help.
→
Time credits and reciprocity tracking
Not money. Recognition that giving and receiving balance over time.
→
Open source, replicable
Any city can run their own Flourish. London builds the template.
Where we are

This is early. The board is live. The SMS line works. Real posts are going up.

We're not trying to scale. We're trying to see if neighbours actually show up for each other when given a simple, trusted place to ask.

So far: they do.
